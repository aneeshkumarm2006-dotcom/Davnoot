FAQ

Honest answers
to hard questions.

How long does a Shopify build take?+
Most custom builds and migrations run five to eight weeks from kickoff to launch, depending on catalog size and how much custom functionality you need. A store audit is one to two weeks. We give you a fixed timeline with the bid, so you're not guessing.
Do I own the store and the theme?+
Completely. It's your Shopify account and your custom theme, with no license fees and nothing locked behind us. If you want to take it in-house or hand it to another agency after launch, you can walk away with everything.
Do I need Shopify Plus, or is standard Shopify enough?+
Most brands do great on standard Shopify. Plus earns its cost when you need checkout customization, Shopify Functions, B2B, high volume, or multiple stores. We'll tell you honestly which one fits, even when the answer is the cheaper one.
Can you migrate me from WooCommerce, Magento, or Wix?+
Yes, that's a large part of what we do. We migrate products, customers, and content, then build a full redirect map so old URLs point to the right new pages. Done right, a migration is a fresh start, not a traffic cliff.
Will I lose my Google rankings when the store changes?+
Not if it's handled properly, and that's exactly why SEO is part of every build. We preserve URL structure where we can, redirect everything else, keep your schema and metadata intact, and monitor after launch. Rankings should hold or improve, not drop.
