FAQ

AI SEO in Montreal,
plainly answered.

What is AI SEO for a Montreal business?+
AI SEO (also called generative engine optimization, or GEO) is the work of getting your business named and cited when AI engines like ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews answer questions from Montreal buyers. Instead of competing for a ranking on a results page, you're competing to be the recommendation inside the answer itself.
How is this different from regular Montreal SEO?+
Traditional SEO optimizes pages for crawlers and rankings. AI SEO optimizes your entire footprint (structured data, reviews, local citations, content structure) for retrieval systems that read, summarize, and cite sources. The two overlap and compound, which is why most Montreal clients run both together.
Do you optimize in French as well as English?+
Yes. Montreal buyers ask AI engines questions in both languages, and the engines answer each from different sources. We baseline, build, and monitor your AI visibility in English and French so you're the answer on both sides of the market.
How long until AI engines start citing my business?+
First citations typically appear within 60–90 days, with consistent visibility across engines for your priority Montreal queries in 4–6 months. We track daily, so you see movement as it happens rather than waiting on a quarterly report.
What kinds of Montreal businesses does this work for?+
Any business whose buyers research before choosing: professional services, clinics, B2B and SaaS companies, agencies, e-commerce, and local services. People increasingly ask AI engines "who's the best X in Montreal," and AI SEO decides whether you're in that answer.
