Why audio wins

Attention with
no scroll-past.

On a feed, your ad competes with everything around it. On Spotify, a single spot plays alone, in the listener's ears, at full attention. That's a different kind of reach.

Spotify audio ads run on the free, ad-supported tier, where a 15 or 30 second spot plays between tracks with nothing to scroll past. Completion rates sit far above video feeds because there's no competing content and no easy skip. It's the closest thing digital has to old-school radio reach, with modern targeting on top.

Podcasts add trust. A host-read sponsorship borrows the credibility the host has built with their audience, and dynamic ad insertion lets you scale that read across shows and episodes. Spotify also reaches the listeners traditional media keeps losing: cord-cutters and younger buyers who skipped cable and terrestrial radio entirely. We build across all of it, then tie it back to your paid ads so audio warms demand your other channels close.

FAQ

Spotify ads,
plainly answered.

Who actually hears Spotify audio ads?+
Audio and video ads serve to Spotify's free, ad-supported listeners, since Premium is ad-free. That's still a large, highly engaged audience, and it skews toward younger, cord-cutting buyers who are hard to reach on broadcast. Podcasts are the exception: their host-read and dynamically inserted ads reach both free and Premium listeners, so a podcast sponsorship covers the whole base.
What's the difference between audio ads and podcast ads?+
Audio ads are your own produced spot, bought through Ad Studio and inserted between tracks on the free tier, with a clickable companion banner. Podcast ads run inside shows: either host-read, where the creator voices your message, or dynamically inserted, where your spot is placed programmatically across episodes. Host-read borrows the host's credibility. Audio ads give you more control over targeting and creative. Most brands run both.
How is Spotify targeting different from Meta or Google?+
Spotify targets the moment, not just the person. Alongside age, gender, and location, you can reach listeners by genre, playlist, device, and real-time activity: working out, commuting, studying, cooking, or winding down. That context is unique to audio. It lets you match the message to what someone is doing, which is why a focus-session ad and a party-playlist ad should never be the same spot.
Do Spotify ads drive conversions or just awareness?+
Both, if you set them up to. Audio's core strength is reach and recall, so it fills the top of the funnel better than almost anything. But with the Spotify Pixel, promo codes, and a retargeting layer on paid social, you can connect that reach to action. We treat Spotify as demand generation that warms audiences your other channels then convert, and we measure it that way.
What's the minimum budget to run Spotify ads?+
Spotify Ad Studio is self-serve with a low entry point, so you can start a test campaign for a few hundred dollars. Meaningful reach and a readable brand-lift signal take more, and premium formats like Video Takeover, Marquee, and larger podcast sponsorships carry higher minimums. We scope the budget to the goal in the audit rather than pushing you to a package.
Can you produce the audio creative in French?+
Yes. We write, cast, and mix in both English and French, which matters for the Montreal market and any bilingual audience. Audio creative is written for the ear from scratch, not translated from a display ad, so each language reads naturally rather than sounding dubbed.
