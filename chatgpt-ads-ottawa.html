Why it matters here

Ottawa researches
with AI assistants.

The capital runs on considered, documented buying: associations, contractors, consultancies, and public-sector-adjacent firms. That buyer researches carefully, often bilingually, and increasingly starts that research inside an AI assistant.

Ottawa's economy skews B2B and professional services, and those buyers don't impulse-purchase. They ask ChatGPT to compare vendors, tell Perplexity to summarize who's credible, and lean on Copilot inside the Microsoft tools they already use at work. By the time a form gets filled, the AI answer has already narrowed the field, and sponsored placement is how you get into that narrowing.

Ottawa is also officially bilingual, so a real share of those prompts come in French and get answered from French sources. We plan ChatGPT ads for both languages and pair them with your AI search visibility so a careful capital buyer keeps seeing you whether they're comparing, verifying, or ready to shortlist.

FAQ

ChatGPT ads in Ottawa,
plainly answered.

Can an Ottawa B2B or professional-services firm run ChatGPT ads now?+
Yes. Sponsored placements are live across Perplexity, Microsoft Copilot, and increasingly ChatGPT's search surfaces, and they reach Ottawa users today. For considered B2B purchases, being the named option inside the AI answer is worth far more than another display impression.
Does bilingual matter for AI ads in Ottawa?+
In the capital, yes. A meaningful share of prompts arrive in French, and AI engines answer those from French sources in a separate auction. We plan and bid English and French placements independently so you cover both sides of the Ottawa market.
Our sales cycle is long. How do you attribute an Ottawa AI ad to a closed deal?+
We build a custom attribution stack designed for slow B2B cycles: server-side conversion tracking, UTM logic, and post-sale surveys that ask how buyers found you. A lead that began in an AI answer still ties back to the placement when it closes weeks or months later.
Should Ottawa firms do this instead of Google or LinkedIn ads?+
No, alongside them. AI ads are early, cheap, high-intent inventory that complements the channels you already run. Ottawa buyers move between Google, LinkedIn, and AI assistants, so the strongest plans cover all of them rather than betting on one.
Do you understand the Ottawa and public-sector-adjacent market?+
Yes. Davnoot works with B2B, association, and professional-services clients, and we buy in both official languages. We tune creative for a risk-aware, documentation-minded capital buyer rather than reusing a generic consumer playbook.
