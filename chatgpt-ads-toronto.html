FAQ

ChatGPT ads in Toronto,
plainly answered.

Why do ChatGPT ads matter more in Toronto than elsewhere?+
Toronto generates the highest volume of local AI queries in Canada, so a single owned prompt can drive real pipeline here. It's also the most competitive market, which means the sponsored slot inside the answer is contested ground worth taking before your rivals do.
The GTA is competitive. Can I still win placements in Toronto?+
Yes, because the inventory is still early and underpriced even in Toronto. We target the prompts where you can win now, secure the placement, and defend it, so you're the cited recommendation before CPMs climb toward Google and Meta levels.
Toronto buyers are diverse and multilingual. How do you handle that?+
We map the many ways a GTA audience phrases the same intent, including different languages, and build creative and bids across that spread. That way you show up whether a buyer asks in plain English, a niche phrasing, or another language entirely.
How do you prove these Toronto ads drove revenue, not just clicks?+
The platforms report poorly, so we build a custom attribution stack: server-side conversion tracking, UTM logic, and post-purchase surveys. Every prompt, engine, and placement ties back to booked GTA revenue rather than an impression count you can't bank.
Should Toronto brands do this instead of Google or Meta ads?+
No, alongside them. AI ads are cheap, high-intent inventory that complements your existing channels rather than replacing them. In a market as competitive as Toronto, the brands running Google, Meta, and AI placements in parallel are the ones that pull ahead.
