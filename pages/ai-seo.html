FAQ

Honest answers
to hard questions.

Is AI SEO actually different from regular SEO?+
Yes — meaningfully. Traditional SEO targets crawlers indexing pages. AI SEO targets retrieval systems that read, summarize, and cite. The signals overlap (quality content, authority, technical health) but the optimization tactics, measurement, and outcomes are different.
How do you measure AI search visibility?+
Daily sampling of priority buyer queries across ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews, and Bing Chat. We track citations earned, position within answers, competitive presence, and traffic referred from AI sources.
Should I do AI SEO instead of traditional SEO?+
Neither — both. Traditional search still drives the vast majority of qualified traffic. AI SEO captures the high-intent, fast-growing share that traditional SEO can't. Most clients run both, often integrated into one engagement.
How long until I'm cited?+
First citations typically appear within 60–90 days. Consistent citations across multiple engines for priority queries take 4–6 months. Like traditional SEO, this is patient work — but with faster feedback loops because we can see results daily.
What if AI engines change their algorithms?+
They will — constantly. Our playbook is built around the durable signals (entity authority, source credibility, structured content) that retrieval systems will keep weighing. Tactic-level changes get reflected within weeks; strategic foundations don't break.
