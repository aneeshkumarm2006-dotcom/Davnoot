01

Strategy & copywriting

Brand voice, narrative arcs, sequence architecture, and copy that actually gets read. Every email earns its place by entertaining, educating, or converting — never just "checking in."

Automated flows

Editorial newsletters that build a real audience. Designed to be forwarded, screenshotted, and looked forward to. The kind of thing people read on a Sunday morning by choice.

Performance analytics

Revenue-attributed reporting across opens, clicks, conversions, and lifetime value lift. We track what email is actually contributing — net new revenue, not last-click claims.

A/B testing

Subject lines, send times, layouts, offers, copy angles — tested systematically with sample sizes that actually mean something. Every test feeds the next month's playbook.

FAQ

Honest answers
to hard questions.

How big does my list need to be?+
10,000 active subscribers is the practical floor for meaningful revenue impact. Below that, list growth becomes the priority before optimization — we'll tell you so honestly.
What ESPs do you work with?+
Klaviyo for ecommerce, Customer.io for SaaS and product-led businesses, Mailchimp only when there's existing setup we shouldn't disrupt. We have strong opinions about platforms — and good reasons for them.
How often will you actually send?+
Frequency is segment-specific. Engaged subscribers get 2–3 emails per week; less engaged segments get less, treated more carefully. We optimize for revenue per subscriber, not send volume.
What if my brand isn't a fit for "salesy" emails?+
Good — neither are we. The best email programs feel like a great editorial newsletter that occasionally sells something, not a discount factory.
How do you handle deliverability if it's bad?+
First 30 days are warming, list cleaning, and authentication setup (SPF, DKIM, DMARC, BIMI). We protect sender reputation aggressively — it's the foundation that lets every other tactic work.
