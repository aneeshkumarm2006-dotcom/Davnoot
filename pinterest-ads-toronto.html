Why Toronto

The biggest market,
the tightest margins.

The GTA is the largest and most contested Pinterest audience in the country. There's more demand than anywhere else in Canada, but also more competition and higher CPMs, so sloppy targeting and reused creative get expensive fast.

Toronto is one of the most multicultural cities on earth, and its Pinterest audience reflects that: fashion, home, weddings, real estate, and food all pull huge, diverse planning traffic. The upside is scale. The catch is that every brand wants the same high-intent planner, which pushes auction prices up and makes broad "spray and pray" targeting a fast way to burn budget.

That's why we lead with precision. A clean product feed, ideally from your Shopify catalog, powers Shopping ads, while sharp keyword and actalike targeting plus disciplined retargeting keep the efficient audiences separate from the expensive ones. Combined with strong native creative, that's how you win a market where paid social on Meta is already crowded and costly.

FAQ

Pinterest Ads in Toronto,
plainly answered.

Are Pinterest Ads worth it in a competitive market like Toronto?+
Yes, if you run them with precision. Toronto has more high-intent planners than anywhere else in Canada, but also more competition and higher CPMs, so broad targeting and recycled creative get expensive. Tight keyword and actalike audiences, disciplined retargeting, and strong native pins are what turn the GTA's scale into profit rather than wasted spend.
Why are Pinterest CPMs higher in the Toronto and GTA market?+
It's simple auction pressure: the GTA is Canada's largest and most contested audience, so more advertisers bid for the same planners and prices rise. The answer isn't to avoid Toronto, it's to be more precise than competitors, spending on high-intent keyword, retargeting, and actalike audiences instead of broad reach that quietly inflates your cost per conversion.
How do you reach Toronto's diverse, multicultural shoppers?+
We segment rather than treat the GTA as one generic buyer. Interest, keyword, and behavioural targeting let us reach distinct communities, and creative is tested per segment so pins actually resonate instead of averaging out. In a city this varied, matching the right creative to the right audience is one of the biggest levers on ROAS.
Which Toronto business categories perform best on Pinterest?+
Fashion, home and decor, weddings, real estate and staging, and food and lifestyle brands lead in the GTA, because Torontonians use Pinterest to plan exactly those purchases. A synced product catalog turns each item into a shoppable, retargetable pin. If your product is impulse-only or purely B2B with no visual angle, other channels usually come first.
How long until Pinterest ads perform for a Toronto brand?+
Conversion campaigns need a couple of weeks to exit the learning phase on clean data. Most GTA accounts reach a workable ROAS by month two or three, and in a high-CPM market that timeline rewards fast, decisive optimization: we cut inefficient segments early and scale into the product groups already winning, so the account gets more efficient over time.
