Why Montreal

A music city that
listens in French and English.

Montreal treats music as culture, not background noise. That gives audio ads more attention here than almost anywhere, but only if the spot speaks the right language at the right moment.

The thing outside agencies miss about Montreal is that it's genuinely two audio markets sharing one map. A listener in Rosemont builds a playlist in French. A student in Mile End streams podcasts in English. The Plateau does both in the same afternoon. Run one English spot at all of it and you sound like a brand from somewhere else. We write and voice separate reads for each side, so the ad sounds like it belongs.

Then there's the calendar. Montreal's summer runs on festivals, from the Jazz Fest to Osheaga to the Francos, and audio is how people soundtrack getting there: the metro ride, the walk down Sainte-Catherine, the pre-game playlist. We build campaigns around those windows and tie the reach back to your paid social so the attention turns into bookings, not just plays.

Capabilities

Every Spotify
format, tuned for Montreal.

Spotify Ad Studio sells more than a pre-roll. We run the full inventory and pick the format that fits your goal and your language mix.

01

Audio spots

15 and 30 second reads on the ad-supported tier with a clickable companion banner. Produced in French, English, or both, and written to hold a Montreal listener through the full spot.

02

Video Takeover

Full-screen video served when a listener is actively looking at the app. The one moment you get both ears and eyes, so we save it for launches and festival-season pushes.

03

Podcast ads

Host-read and dynamically inserted spots across Spotify's podcast network, including the French-language Quebec shows most agencies skip. We match on audience fit, not download counts.

04

Playlist and genre targeting

Reach listeners by genre, mood, playlist, activity, and device. A focus-session read and a workout read are never the same spot, and neither is a French one and an English one.

05

Sequential messaging

Order the story across a flight so a listener hears the intro, then the offer, then the reminder, instead of the same 30 seconds on repeat until it wears out.

06

Measurement and tracking

Spotify Pixel, promo codes, reach and frequency, and brand-lift studies where volume allows, all tied to the visits and signups that matter to your Montreal business.

Our approach

From silence
to share of ear.

Audio rewards a clear plan before launch. Get the moment, the language, and the message right first, and the optimization has something worth scaling.

01
Days 1–14 · Plan

Map the Montreal moments.

We match your buyer to the contexts they listen in, commute, workout, focus, festival, and decide the French and English split before a dollar of media moves.

02
Days 15–30 · Produce

Write for both ears.

Separate scripts voiced and mixed in French and English, each built around one idea. Podcast reads briefed so Quebec hosts sound like themselves, not like a banner read aloud.

03
Days 31–60 · Launch

Go live, then tune.

Campaigns launch in Ad Studio and across podcast inventory. We watch completion, frequency, and response by language, then shift budget toward the moments and shows that pull.

04
Day 60+ · Scale

Prove it, then push it.

Brand lift and pixel attribution tell us what worked in each language. We scale the winning moments, refresh creative before wear-out, and feed warmed listeners into retargeting.

FAQ

Spotify ads in Montreal,
plainly answered.

Can you run Spotify ads in French for Montreal?+
Yes, and we treat it as the default, not an add-on. We write, cast, and mix separate French and English spots so each sounds native to a Montreal listener rather than dubbed. You can run one language, both split by playlist and audience, or sequence them, and we report performance for each side so you see which half of the market is converting.
Are you actually based in Montreal?+
We are. Davnoot is a Montreal agency, so we plan around this city's calendar and language split from the inside rather than guessing at it. That means we already know which festival windows, transit patterns, and French podcast shows are worth buying, and we can meet in person when it helps.
Do Spotify ads work for a small Montreal business?+
They can, because Ad Studio is self-serve with a low entry point, so you can test a Montreal campaign for a few hundred dollars. We tighten the targeting to your neighbourhoods and moments so a modest budget still buys real frequency against the right listeners instead of spreading thin across the province.
How do you target Montreal listeners specifically?+
We layer location on top of the moment: genre, playlist, activity, device, and time of day. For Montreal that usually means commuter and metro windows, workout and focus sessions, and festival-season spikes, split across French and English audiences so the spot matches both the place and the person.
Can you tie Spotify ads in Montreal to real sales?+
Yes. Audio's strength is reach and recall, but we connect it to action with the Spotify Pixel, promo codes, and a retargeting layer on paid social. We treat Spotify as demand generation that warms Montreal audiences your other channels then convert, and we report visits and signups rather than stopping at plays.
