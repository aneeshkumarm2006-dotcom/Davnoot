Why it works

Where intent lives
before the search.

On most ad platforms you interrupt people. On Pinterest you meet them while they are actively planning a purchase, which changes the economics of every click.

People do not open Pinterest to be entertained. They open it to plan a kitchen remodel, a capsule wardrobe, a wedding, a dinner, a nursery. That planning mindset means an ad is not an interruption. It is part of the research, which is why Pinterest consistently converts for considered, visual purchases.

It also behaves differently than paid social on Meta. Pins have a long shelf life, so a saved pin can keep sending you buyers months after the spend. Pair that with a clean product feed, ideally from your Shopify catalog, and Shopping ads plus dynamic retargeting do the compounding work that saves and impressions never will.

FAQ

Pinterest Ads,
plainly answered.

Is Pinterest Ads worth it for my business?+
If you sell something people plan and picture before buying, yes. Pinterest is strongest for home and decor, fashion, beauty, food, wedding, travel, and DIY, because those buyers use the platform to research purchases. If your product is impulse-only or purely B2B with no visual angle, other channels usually come first.
How is Pinterest different from Meta or Google Ads?+
Meta interrupts entertainment and Google captures active search. Pinterest sits in between: people are in a planning, high-intent mindset but earlier in the journey. Pins also have a long lifespan, so spend keeps compounding after launch, and competition is lighter in many categories, which can mean lower CPMs than Meta for the same audience.
Do I need a product catalog or Shopify to advertise?+
You can run standard and video pins without one, but a catalog unlocks the best performance. It powers Shopping ads and dynamic retargeting, where every product becomes a shoppable, personalized ad. If you are on Shopify the feed connects cleanly, and it is usually the first thing we set up.
Which ad formats actually perform on Pinterest?+
For ecommerce, Shopping ads from your catalog and Collections ads tend to drive the most efficient conversions, since they move people from browse straight to product. Idea and video ads build reach and consideration, and standard pins carry evergreen offers. We match the format to the funnel stage rather than betting everything on one.
How do you track conversions and prove ROAS?+
We install the Pinterest Tag and the server-side Conversions API, then deduplicate events so checkouts and leads report accurately even when browsers block pixels. Campaigns optimize toward conversions or catalog sales, and reporting is built around ROAS and product-group performance, not saves or impressions.
How long until Pinterest ads perform?+
Conversion campaigns need a couple of weeks to exit the learning phase on clean data. Most accounts see a workable ROAS by month two and three, and it tends to improve from there as pins age, audiences mature, and we scale into the product groups that are already winning.
